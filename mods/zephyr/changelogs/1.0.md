## Additions

- Support for the English and German language

**Removed and Unused Content**
- Unused paintings like Earth, Fire, Water and Wind are now obtainable in survival
- Added Petrified Slabs for every Wood Type with a respective recipe using the specific wood slab and a stone slab
- Added a new grass-like plant "Clovers", that spawns in patches similar to bushes and has a small chance to drop either a Clover or a Rotten Clover which can be used to brew Luck and Bad Luck Potions
- The wither rose can be used to brew the Potion of Decay
[gray]*Because of this the Luck, Bad Luck and Wither Effect have been added to the advancement "A Furious Cocktail" and the Luck and Bad Luck Effect also to "How Did We Get Here?".*[/]

**Overlooked Bugs**
- Fixed the Ominous Banner on Outposts, whose patterns were visible in the tooltip despite not being intended to show
- Placed the missing ladder in the trial chamber atrium_1
- Placed the 2 missing tuff blocks under the fountain in atrium_1

**Underutilized Mechanics**
- Enchantment level symbols now go up to the limit (255)
- The Wind Burst enchantment can now appear from levels 1-3 in an ominous vault
- The textures of shields with banners are now no longer shrunk down
- Replaced the old end city banner with a new reworked "Void Banner" that is also available in the Creative Menu and more in line with the Ominous Banner
- Bone meal can now be used on sugar cane, cactus, nether warts, small flower and pumpkin and melon stems to instantly grow pumpkins or melons
