## Additions:

**Shifting Book**
- Interacting with the Shifting Book will grand you a random Enchanted Book
- Interacting with the Shifting Book has a cost of 5 levels and a cooldown of 3 seconds that can be changed in the configs
- Can be stacked up to 16 items per stack
- Can be crafted using 3 random Enchanted Books and an Amethyst Shard

**Loot Tables**
- Added a loot tables for the Shifting Book with specific chances of the enchantment levels
[gray]Chances: Level I - 40%, Level II - 30%, Level III - 15%, Level IV - 10%, Level 5 - 4%, Treasure Enchantments - 1%[/]

**Advancements**
- Added the "Shifting Knowledge" advancement for interacting with the Shifting Book successfully

## Changes:

- Anvils no longer have the cap of 40 levels
- Shifting Books can now be put into Chiseled Bookshelves

**Frost Walker Enchantment**
- The base radius of the ice has been increased to 4.0 instead of 3.0
- The radius addition per level has been increased to 2.0 instead of 1.0
[gray]*This change was made to make sprint-jumping on water while using Frost Walker possible.*[/]
