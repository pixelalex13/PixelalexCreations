## Additions

- TNT can now be defused by interacting with it using shears
- Defused TNT can now be rearmed by interacting with it using gun powder
- New spawn eggs for the Illusioner, Giant and Mannequin are available in the creative menu
- The Ender Dragon and Wither spawn eggs are now available in the creative menu
- A Eye Sensor block that gives out a redstone signal relative from how close to the center of the block you look
- A crafting recipe for the Eye Sensor block consisting of end stone, echo shards, redstone dust and an eye of ender
